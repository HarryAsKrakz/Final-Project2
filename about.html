Welcome To Kay's Crochets

Welcome to my website. You will find dozens of products hand crafted to perfection!


We are a small, family-owned and operated business. We take pride in our work, and we love what we do. We are passionate about crocheting. We are committed to providing the highest quality products and services possible. We believe in old-fashioned values like hard work and customer service. We are dedicated to providing the best possible experience for our customers. Thank you for choosing us!

Crocheting is a huge passion of ours and we love nothing more than being able to share that with others. It brings us joy to see the look on someone's face when we finish a project that they thought was impossible. We started this business as a way to do just that. To share our love of crocheting with others. We have years of experience crocheting and love teaching others how to do it too.
